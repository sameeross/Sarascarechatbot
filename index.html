About the Festival

Welcome to the Melbourne Diwali Mela at Marvel Stadium! Join us for a dazzling celebration of the Festival of Lights, bringing the warmth, color, and spirit of Diwali to the heart of Melbourne. This grand community event is designed for everyone, regardless of background, to experience the richness of Indian culture.
